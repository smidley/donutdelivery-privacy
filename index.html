Donut Delivery — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026
Short version: Donut Delivery does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game has no ads, no analytics, no accounts, and no tracking. It does not connect to the internet. Everything stays on your device.

Who this policy is for

This policy explains how the mobile game Donut Delivery (the “App”) handles information. The App is designed as a family-friendly game suitable for young children. Because children may play it, we have built it to collect no personal data at all.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children. Specifically, the App does not:

  • ask for or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information;
  • require an account, sign-in, or registration;
  • collect location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data;
  • use advertising, ad networks, or in-app purchases;
  • use analytics, tracking, or third-party software development kits (SDKs);
  • collect device identifiers for advertising or tracking purposes.

Information stored on your device

To let you keep playing where you left off, the App saves your game progress (for example: coins earned, the current day, garden crops, and sound/music settings) locally on your device only, using the device’s standard app storage. This information never leaves your device, is not personal, and is not sent to us or to anyone else. Deleting the App removes this saved data.

Internet and network use

The App does not connect to the internet and does not transmit any data over a network. It works fully offline.

Children’s privacy

We take children’s privacy seriously. Donut Delivery is intended to be safe for children to play. Consistent with the U.S. Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, including provisions for children), and Apple’s Kids Category guidelines, the App collects no personal information from children (or anyone). There is nothing to delete on our end, because we never receive any data.

Third parties

The App contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or social media components, and does not share any information with third parties.

Changes to this policy

If the App’s data practices ever change (for example, if a future version adds an online feature), we will update this policy and the “Last updated” date above before that feature ships. Continued data practices will always be disclosed here.
